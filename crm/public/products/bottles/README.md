# Product Bottle Images

Place PNG images for water bottles here.

## Required Images:

- **19l.png** - 19 liter water bottle icon
- **10l.png** - 10 liter water bottle icon
- **5l.png** - 5 liter water bottle icon
- **0.5l.png** - 0.5-1.5 liter bottle icon

## Image Specifications:

- **Format:** PNG with transparency
- **Size:** 512x512px (recommended)
- **Aspect:** Square or vertical
- **Background:** Transparent
- **File Size:** < 100KB each

## Where to Get Images:

1. **Custom Design:** Create in Figma or Adobe Illustrator
2. **Icon Packs:** Flaticon, Icons8 (commercial license)
3. **Stock Photos:** Unsplash, Pexels (with background removed)
4. **3D Renders:** Simple 3D bottle models

## Example Sources:

- https://www.flaticon.com/search?word=water+bottle
- https://icons8.com/icons/set/water-bottle
- https://www.remove.bg (to remove backgrounds)

---

**Note:** Images are optional. Products will display correctly even without images. Images enhance the visual experience in the CRM interface.
